Stay Safe Online – Advanced Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

🔐 Account Security
- Use strong, unique passwords for every account (minimum 8–12 characters with symbols)
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
- Never share passwords or OTPs with anyone, even if they claim to be officials

🌐 Safe Browsing Practices
- Always verify website URLs before entering sensitive information
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links in emails or messages
- Check for “https://” and security lock icon in websites

📧 Email & Message Awareness
- Do not trust messages that create urgency (e.g., “account will be blocked”)
- Verify sender email addresses carefully (fake emails may look similar)
- Avoid downloading attachments from unknown sources

💻 Device & Network Safety
- Keep your mobile and computer software updated regularly
- Install antivirus software and keep it active
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or personal transactions

💰 UPI & Financial Safety
- Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps
- Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers
- Always double-check receiver details before making payments

🧠 Smart Digital Behavior
- Think before sharing personal information online
- Avoid oversharing details on social media
- Stay informed about common cyber scams

🚨 Reporting & Emergency Action
- Immediately report cyber fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (India)
- Inform your bank immediately in case of suspicious transactions

✔ Remember: Awareness and caution are the best defenses against cybercrime.
